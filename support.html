Equipment & Lookup
üîç How Does Equipment Lookup Work? ‚ñº

Enter a model number in the lookup tool and EquipAI will match it against a built-in database of 70+ major manufacturer prefixes. When a match is found, specifications like BTU rating, efficiency ratings, and compatible parts are automatically filled in.

You can also use the Enhanced Lookup option to connect manufacturer APIs for even more detailed data. API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain.
