Privacy Policy

Exactajob

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Exactajob. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Exactajob, you may provide:

  • Property Information: Property names, addresses, construction year, and square footage
  • Room Measurements: Room dimensions, ceiling heights, window and door counts
  • Job Details: Job names, types, and specifications
  • Client Information: Optional client names, email addresses, and phone numbers for your records

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version (for crash reporting and compatibility)
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about app features used (anonymous and aggregated)

Location Information

If you choose to use the property lookup or map measurement features:

  • We access the addresses you enter to retrieve property data from public records
  • Location services may be used to center maps on your property
  • You can control location permissions in your device Settings

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Store your properties, rooms, and jobs locally on your device
  • Generate Estimates: Calculate cost estimates based on your measurements and specifications
  • Property Lookups: Retrieve property data from public records when you use the lookup feature
  • Map Features: Display satellite imagery and measurement tools for roofing and insulation jobs
  • Improve the App: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your property data, room measurements, and job details are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure data storage:

  • Data is encrypted at rest using iOS encryption
  • Data is backed up to iCloud (if you have iCloud Backup enabled)
  • We do not upload your property or job data to our servers

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RentCast API: For property data lookups. Only the address you enter is sent to retrieve public record information. This service is rate-limited to 2 lookups per user.
  • Apple MapKit: For displaying maps and satellite imagery. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With Your Consent: When you share PDF estimates via email or other apps
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate the app (e.g., API providers) under strict confidentiality agreements

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Edit Your Data: You can edit or delete properties, rooms, and jobs at any time
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Permissions

You can control app permissions in iOS Settings:

  • Location Services: Required for map features and property lookup
  • Camera (if applicable): Not currently used

Children's Privacy

Exactajob is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device as long as the app is installed. When you delete the app:

  • All local data is permanently deleted
  • iCloud backups may retain data according to your iCloud settings
  • Cached property lookup results are cleared

International Users

Exactajob is designed for users in the United States. If you use the app from outside the U.S., your data will be stored on your device in your location. The property lookup feature uses U.S. property databases and may not work for international addresses.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing a notice when you open the app (for significant changes)

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@exactajob.com
Website: www.exactajob.com

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about data we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@exactajob.com

EU/UK Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • iOS encryption for data at rest
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all network communications
  • Limited data collection (only what's necessary for app functionality)
  • No cloud storage of sensitive property data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Consent
