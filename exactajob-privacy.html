Privacy Policy for Exactajob

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Exactajob ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Exactajob, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Property Information

  • Property addresses you enter
  • Room measurements and dimensions
  • Project names and descriptions
  • Photos you add to properties or projects
  • Notes and custom data you create

Usage Information

  • Number of property lookups performed
  • Projects created and their status
  • App preferences and settings

Information We Collect Automatically

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (only for app functionality)

Location Information

  • We do NOT collect your precise location
  • Property addresses are only used when you explicitly enter them for lookups
  • No background location tracking

Information from Third-Party Services

RentCast API

  • When you look up a property address, we send that address to RentCast API
  • RentCast returns public property information (square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms, year built)
  • We do not control RentCast's data practices. See their privacy policy at: https://www.rentcast.io/privacy

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

App Functionality

  • Store your property measurements and project data on your device
  • Perform property lookups using the RentCast API
  • Calculate cost estimates for your projects
  • Generate PDF reports
  • Sync data across your devices (if using iCloud)

App Improvement

  • Understand how features are used (aggregate data only)
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app performance

Communication

  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important app updates (if you opt in)

How We Store Your Information

Local Storage

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Property measurements, projects, photos, and notes are saved in the app's local database
  • We do NOT store your data on our servers

iCloud Sync (Optional)

  • If you enable iCloud, your data syncs across your Apple devices
  • iCloud data is encrypted and controlled by Apple
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • See Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

API Keys

  • RentCast API keys are embedded in the app using obfuscation
  • Keys are never transmitted to our servers
  • API calls go directly from your device to RentCast

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services We Use

RentCast API

  • Purpose: Property data lookups
  • What's shared: Only the property address you enter
  • Their privacy policy: https://www.rentcast.io/privacy

Apple Services

  • iCloud (optional): For data sync across your devices
  • App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • Responding to subpoenas or court orders
  • Protecting our rights or property
  • Investigating fraud or security issues

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

  • All your data is accessible within the app
  • Export your project data as PDFs
  • View all stored properties and measurements

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual properties, projects, or rooms at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • If using iCloud, delete data from iCloud settings

Opt-Out Options

  • Location Services: We don't use location services
  • iCloud Sync: Disable in app settings or iOS Settings
  • Notifications: Manage in iOS Settings → Notifications

Data Portability

  • Export your project estimates as PDFs
  • Share data with contractors or other apps

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Local encryption: Data stored on your device is protected by iOS security
  • Secure transmission: All API calls use HTTPS encryption
  • No cloud storage: We don't store your data on our servers
  • API key obfuscation: API keys are obfuscated (not plain text)

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Exactajob is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@exactajob.com.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Delete your personal information (by uninstalling the app)
  • Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@exactajob.com.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@exactajob.com.

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Consent: You provide information voluntarily
  • Contract: To provide the app services you requested
  • Legitimate interests: To improve the app and provide support

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • iCloud data: Retained according to your iCloud settings
  • API usage: We don't store logs of your API calls
  • Support requests: Retained for as long as necessary to resolve your issue

Your continued use of Exactajob after changes means you accept the updated policy.

  • We comply with applicable data protection laws
