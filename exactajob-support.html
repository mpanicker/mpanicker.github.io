Getting Started

Welcome to Exactajob! Follow these simple steps to create your first cost estimate:

1Create a Property

Start by adding your property with basic information:

  • Property name (e.g., "Smith Residence")
  • Full address
  • Year built
  • Optional: Total square footage

2Add Rooms

Add rooms to your property by entering:

  • Room name (e.g., "Living Room", "Kitchen")
  • Width and length in feet
  • Ceiling height
  • Number of windows

Tip: Edit your property anytime to add, edit, or delete rooms.

3Create a Job

From your property detail screen, tap "Create Job" and select your job type:

HVAC Heating and cooling system installation
Flooring Hardwood, carpet, tile, vinyl, laminate
Windows Window replacement and installation
Roofing Roof repair and replacement
Insulation Attic, wall, and crawlspace insulation
Plumbing Pipe work and fixture installation

4Enter Job Details

Provide specific information about your project:

  • For Flooring: Select which rooms to floor, material type, and quality level
  • For Windows: Add each window size, type, and specifications
  • For HVAC: System type, number of units, and installation requirements
  • For Roofing: Roof type, pitch, and square footage (measure on map)
  • For Insulation: Area to insulate, R-value, and square footage
  • For Plumbing: Work type, fixtures, and pipe details

5Get Your Estimate

The app will generate a detailed cost estimate including:

  • Labor costs
  • Material costs
  • Equipment costs (if applicable)
  • Total project cost

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add rooms to my property?
Navigate to your property detail screen, tap the menu (three dots), and select "Edit Property". From there, you can add rooms by tapping "Add Room" and entering the dimensions. You can also edit or delete existing rooms.
Can I create a job without a property?
No, you must create a property first. This ensures accurate estimates based on actual property dimensions. The simplified workflow helps organize all jobs under their respective properties.
How accurate are the cost estimates?
Our estimates use industry-standard calculations based on typical labor rates, material costs, and common project requirements. However, actual costs may vary based on your location, specific materials chosen, contractor rates, and market conditions. We recommend using estimates as a starting point and getting quotes from licensed contractors.
How do I select rooms for a flooring job?
When creating a flooring job, you'll see a list of all rooms in your property. Simply tap the checkbox next to each room you want to include in the flooring estimate. The app will automatically calculate the total area and provide accurate pricing based on your selections.
Can I edit a property after creating jobs for it?
Yes! You can edit your property details and room measurements at any time. However, estimates already generated will not automatically update. You may want to create a new estimate if you've made significant changes to room dimensions.
How do I delete a room?
When editing a property, you can delete a room in three ways: (1) Swipe left on the room and tap "Delete", (2) Long-press the room and select "Delete Room" from the context menu, or (3) Tap the red "Delete Room" button at the bottom of the room card.
What job types are supported?
Exactajob currently supports six job types: HVAC (heating/cooling systems), Flooring (hardwood, carpet, tile, vinyl, laminate), Windows (replacement and installation), Roofing (repair and replacement), Insulation (attic, walls, crawlspace), and Plumbing (pipes and fixtures).
How do I share an estimate with a contractor?
From the project detail screen, view your estimate and tap the share button to generate a PDF. You can then email it to contractors or share it via other apps. The PDF includes all project details, measurements, and cost breakdowns.
Can I have multiple properties?
Yes! You can create and manage as many properties as you need. This is useful if you own multiple homes, manage rental properties, or are planning improvements for different locations.
How do I use the roof measurement map?
When creating a roofing or attic insulation job, tap "Measure on Satellite Map". The app will show your property from above. Trace the outline of your roof by tapping to place points around the perimeter. The app automatically calculates the square footage based on your tracing and the selected roof pitch.
What if I don't know my property's square footage?
No problem! The square footage field is optional when creating a property. The app will automatically calculate total square footage by adding up all your room measurements. You can also manually enter a total square footage later if you have that information.
Why do I need to enter room dimensions manually?
Manual entry ensures accuracy and gives you complete control over your property data. You can measure rooms yourself or use existing floor plans. This approach works on all devices and provides reliable data for cost calculations.

Troubleshooting

App Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force quit the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you're running the latest version of iOS

Estimates Seem Inaccurate

  • Double-check all room measurements for accuracy
  • Verify you've selected the correct material types and quality levels
  • Remember that estimates are based on average costs and may vary by region
  • Consider getting quotes from local contractors for comparison

Map Not Loading

  • Ensure location services are enabled for Exactajob
  • Check that you have a stable internet connection
  • Try entering a more complete address (including city and state)
  • Give the app permission to access location services in Settings

Can't Create a Job

  • Make sure you've created a property first
  • Verify the property has at least one room (for most job types)
  • Check that all required fields are filled out
  • Ensure the property has valid measurements

Data Not Saving

  • Make sure you tap "Save" before dismissing any edit screens
  • Check that your device has sufficient storage space
  • Try force-quitting and reopening the app
  • Ensure you're not in Low Power Mode which may limit background activity

System Requirements

  • Devices: iPhone and iPad

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data:

Data Storage

  • All property and job data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload your property information to our servers
  • Your measurements and estimates remain private

Property Lookups

  • When you use the property lookup feature, we query public record APIs
  • Only the address you enter is sent to retrieve property data
  • Property lookup data is cached locally to avoid repeated API calls
  • You have 2 free property lookups (cached lookups don't count)

No Account Required

  • Exactajob does not require account creation
  • No personal information is collected
  • No tracking or analytics beyond basic crash reporting

Tips & Best Practices

For Accurate Estimates

  • Measure rooms carefully using a tape measure
  • Round to the nearest foot for room dimensions
  • Count all windows and doors in each room
  • Use the property lookup feature to get accurate square footage
  • Select material types and quality levels that match your actual plans

Organizing Your Properties

  • Use descriptive property names (e.g., "Main Residence", "Rental - Oak Street")
  • Enter complete addresses for map features to work properly
  • Add all rooms to your property before creating jobs
  • Update room measurements if you discover they were incorrect

Working with Contractors

  • Share your PDF estimates with multiple contractors for competitive bids
  • Use estimates as a baseline to evaluate contractor quotes
  • Discuss any significant differences between your estimate and contractor quotes
  • Remember that estimates are starting points, not guaranteed prices

Planning Multiple Projects

  • Create separate jobs for each project type
  • Use different property names or notes to track renovation phases
  • Compare estimates to prioritize projects by cost
  • Update job status as projects progress
