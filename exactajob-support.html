Setting Up Your First Property

1. Add a Property

  • Tap the "+" button on the Properties tab
  • Enter your property address
  • Optionally use "Look Up Property" to get details from public records
  • Note: You get 2 free property lookups, after which property details must be entered manually

2. Look Up Property Details

Tap "Look Up Property" to fetch:

  • Square footage
  • Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
  • Year built
  • Property location on map

Once looked up, property data is cached and doesn't count toward your lookup limit.

3. Add Rooms

  • Go to the Rooms tab for your property
  • Tap "+" to add a room
  • Enter room name and dimensions
  • Add walls, windows, doors, and fixtures
  • The app automatically calculates square footage

4. Create a Project

  • Go to the Projects tab
  • Tap "+" to create a new project
  • Select rooms to include
  • View estimated costs for paint and labor
  • Generate a PDF to share with contractors

Property Lookups

Q: How many property lookups do I get?
A: You get 2 free property lookups per account. Each unique address lookup counts as one use. Once you've looked up a property, it's cached and you can access it without using additional lookups.
Q: What happens after I use my 2 lookups?
A: You can still use all app features! You'll just need to manually enter property details (square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms) instead of using the automatic lookup. Future updates may offer additional lookup purchases.
Q: Can I look up the same property multiple times?
A: Yes! Once you've looked up a property, it's cached. Subsequent views of that property don't count toward your lookup limit.
Q: My property lookup failed. What should I do?
A: Property lookups require:
  • A valid US address
  • An active internet connection
  • The address to exist in public records

If lookup fails, you can manually enter property details instead. The rest of the app works without property lookups.

Q: Does a failed lookup count against my limit?
A: No, only successful lookups count toward your 2-lookup limit.

Measurements & Rooms

Q: How do I measure my rooms accurately?
Best practices:
  • Measure in feet (decimals are supported, e.g., 12.5 feet)
  • Measure wall to wall, excluding baseboards
  • For ceiling height, measure floor to ceiling
  • Count windows and doors accurately
  • Add notes for irregular features
Q: Can I edit room measurements after saving?
A: Yes! Tap on any room to edit its measurements, add/remove walls, windows, or doors at any time.
Q: What if my room has an unusual shape?
A: Break complex rooms into rectangular sections. You can create multiple "rooms" for one actual room, or add notes about unusual features.
Q: Do I need to measure every room?
A: No, only measure rooms included in your project. If you're only painting the living room, you only need to measure that room.

Cost Estimates

Q: How accurate are the cost estimates?

Our estimates use industry-standard calculations:

  • Paint coverage: ~350 sq ft per gallon
  • Labor rates: Based on national averages
  • Material costs: Typical retail prices

Actual costs vary by:

  • Your geographic location
  • Current material prices
  • Contractor rates in your area
  • Project complexity
  • Season and demand

Use estimates as a baseline for budgeting and comparing contractor quotes.

Q: Can I customize the cost calculations?
A: Currently, the app uses standard rates. Future versions may allow custom pricing. You can use the measurements to create your own estimates with local prices.
Q: Are labor costs included?
A: Yes, estimates include both materials (paint, supplies) and labor costs based on industry standards.
Q: Why are estimates different from contractor quotes?
Many factors affect actual quotes:
  • Local market rates
  • Contractor experience and reputation
  • Project timeline
  • Prep work required
  • Quality of materials
  • Travel distance

Use Exactajob estimates to evaluate if contractor quotes are in a reasonable range.

üõ† Troubleshooting Guide

Problem: Property Lookup Not Working

Check:

  • ‚úÖ Internet connection is active
  • ‚úÖ Address is valid and in the United States
  • ‚úÖ Address includes street number, street name, city, state, ZIP
  • ‚úÖ You have lookups remaining (check Properties tab)

Try:

  • Verify the address is correct (check Google Maps)
  • Use a more specific address format
  • Wait a moment and try again
  • Manually enter property details if lookup continues to fail

Problem: App Running Slowly

Solutions:

  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Delete old projects you no longer need
  • Remove unused properties
  • Update to the latest version
  • Check available storage space

Problem: PDF Won't Generate or Open

Solutions:

  • Ensure project has rooms with measurements
  • Check device storage space
  • Try generating again
  • Share PDF to Files app first, then open
  • Update iOS to latest version
  • Contact support with screenshot of error
