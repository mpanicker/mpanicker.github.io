EquipAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Data Collection

EquipAI collects and stores the following information locally:

  • Equipment information: Models, serial numbers, and installation dates.
  • Documentation: Inspection photos and generated reports.
  • Location data: Equipment placement details (note: we do not track user location).
  • Intelligence: AI-generated diagnostic information.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • Photos are stored using iOS secure storage.
  • Optional: iCloud sync is available only if manually enabled by you.

Data Usage

  • Equipment data is used strictly for tracking and management.
  • Photos are analyzed using on-device AI only.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • No advertising or analytics trackers are included.

User Rights

  • Full control over your data at all times.
  • Ability to delete equipment and inspections anytime.
  • No account registration required.
  • Your data remains your own, on your hardware.

On-Device AI

  • Utilizes Apple Intelligence (on-device processing).
  • Photos never leave your device for processing.
  • AI analysis happens locally with zero cloud processing.
